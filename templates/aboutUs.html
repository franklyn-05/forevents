About Us


Welcome to 4events. Here you will find a collection of all your favourite performers' upcoming shows. View your events and follow your friends to keep up to date with the latest events your friends are attending!

Designed and created by Franklyn Abifarin, this web app allowed me to explore full stack development with Django, using python for back-end, complimented by a combination of HTML, CSS and JavaScript for the front end structure and styling. I am developing an app that put a more social spin on concert websites, eventually facilitating social profiles, with followers and booked events.
